# Project Overview

## Project
**India E-commerce Brand B MMM Case Study**

## Objective
Use Google Meridian to estimate channel contribution, ROI, marginal ROI, response curves, and constrained budget optimization opportunities for Brand B.

## Business Context
Brand B needed clearer, data-backed guidance on how to improve media productivity without assuming that historical allocation was optimal. The goal was not only to measure past channel performance, but to identify where the next unit of budget is most likely to generate incremental value.

## Scope
- **Brand:** Brand B only
- **Market:** India
- **Period:** Jul 2, 2022 – Jun 28, 2025
- **Primary outputs:**
  - contribution decomposition,
  - ROI and marginal ROI interpretation,
  - response-curve saturation insights,
  - fixed-budget optimization recommendations.

## Headline Findings
- Model fit metrics were strong (R²: 0.99, MAPE: 2%, wMAPE: 2%).
- Revenue mix showed large baseline and Trade_Spend influence, with meaningful but heterogeneous media effects.
- Under fixed budget and practical constraints, reallocation suggested ~115K incremental revenue upside.

## Why This Case Study Matters
This project demonstrates the ability to bridge advanced MMM methods with business decision quality:
- framing the right strategic question,
- improving model reliability through thoughtful variable handling,
- translating outputs into realistic budget actions and caveats.
